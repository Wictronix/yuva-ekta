### 1. HOME — `index.html`

#### 1.1 Navbar
- Logo (left)
- Nav links: Home | About | Projects | Impact | Donate | Contact

#### 1.2 Hero Section
- Full-width background image (children in class / community activity)

#### 1.3 Impact Strip
- Full-width dark/brand background band

  - `100` Children Fed Daily
  - `7+` Years of Service

- Short paragraph about YEIF
- Highlight: Founded by Balram Kumar, mentored by Dr. Kiran Bedi

#### 1.5 Key Focus Areas
- 4 icon cards in a grid:
  - 📚 Remedial Education
  - 💻 Digital Literacy
  - 👩 Women's Livelihood
  - 🍱 Health & Nutrition
- Each card: icon + title + 1-line description

#### 1.6 Featured Projects (2-column cards)
- 4 project preview cards
- Each: cover image, project name, supporter name, 2-line description, **"Learn More"** link
- Links to anchor sections on projects.html

#### 1.7 How to Help
- 3 columns with icons:
  - 💰 Donate — make a one-time or monthly gift
  - 🛍️ Buy Products — purchase goods made by beneficiaries
  - 🙋 Volunteer — give your time and skills
- Each has a CTA button

#### 1.8 Quote / Testimonial
- Large pull quote from Balram Kumar (founder)
- Or a community member story (1 line + name + location)
- Simple, centred layout with decorative quotation mark

#### 1.9 Partners & Supporters
- Logo/name row: Morning Star Foundation | Srujna Charitable Trust | Taj Gateway Resort | Expert Solutions for Non Profit
- Grey tinted, simple horizontal scroll on mobile

#### 1.10 CTA Banner
- Bold colour band (brand green or pink)
- Text: "Together we can change more lives."
- Single CTA: **"Donate Today"**

#### 1.11 Footer
- Logo + 1-line tagline

- Contact info column (address, phone, email)
- Social media icons (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube)
